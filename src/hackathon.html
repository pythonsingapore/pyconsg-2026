How It Works

Simple steps from idea to demo

No matter your level, you can contribute. Pick a scope that fits the time you have and the skills you’re growing.

01
Find your team

Bring friends, join a team on-site, or pair up with someone you just met at a talk or workshop.

02
Choose a small, shippable scope

Pick one outcome you can complete: a prototype, a demo, a small tool, a proof-of-concept, or a polished feature.

03
Build in public

Ask for help, share progress, and learn from how other teams approach problems.

04
Demo what you made

Show the room your result, your learnings, and what you’d do next with more time.

Good To Know
Respect and inclusion

Be kind, be curious, and make space for newcomers. If you’re experienced, help someone ship their first thing.

Always applies: Code of Conduct
Ideas That Work Well
  • A tiny internal tool you’ve always wanted
  • A helper script that saves time every week
  • A dashboard or data exploration notebook
  • A small agent workflow that solves one task reliably
  • An open-source contribution sprint
From Second Brain To Showcase

If you’re joining the AI Agent workshop, the hackathon is a natural next step: take what you built, extend it with a feature you care about, and ship a demo.

The best hackathon projects aren’t the biggest — they’re the clearest. One sharp problem. One focused outcome. One demo the room understands instantly.
