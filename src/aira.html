AI Ready ASEAN

In-depth AI Literacy Certification by AI Singapore at PyCon Singapore

In partnership with AI Singapore, PyCon Singapore will run an in-depth, 12-hour equivalent of the AI Ready ASEAN AI literacy programme. The programme is initiated by ASEAN Foundation with support from Google.org, and aims to empower 5.5 million ASEAN citizens to thrive in the AI era.

How PyCon Singapore Runs It

As a community developer conference, PyCon Singapore focuses on AI Usage & Implementation. Participants learn by doing: understanding and applying modern prompting techniques while building a personal AI agent that is useful for their own workflows.

AI fundamentals and AI ethics, privacy, and security are infused throughout the hands-on work, rather than treated as standalone modules. This keeps the content grounded in practical choices participants must make while building.

Learning By Doing

Integrated, hands-on, with clear outcomes

The training is designed to help participants go beyond awareness (AI literacy) into competent use (AI fluency). Expect to practice, reflect, and iterate.

What You'll Practice
  • Modern prompting techniques for higher quality and more reliable outputs
  • Breaking a goal into steps an agent can execute
  • Evaluating results and tightening instructions based on evidence
What You'll Take Home
  • A small personal AI agent workflow tailored to your use case
  • A set of prompts and patterns you can reuse beyond the conference
  • A practical checklist for ethics, privacy, and security considerations

We are excited and can't wait to see what participants will build next.
