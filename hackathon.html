Choose Your Track

Primary Track

Job & Skills Track

Turn public jobs-skills datasets into practical tools. Build career pathway explorers, role-to-skill maps, skills-gap analysers, or learning action planners to help people navigate their upskilling journey.

Download SkillsFuture Dataset

Suggested Build Ideas

  • Career Pathfinder: Adjacent roles via transferable skills.
  • Skills Gap Mapper: Target and rank key missing skills.
  • Learning Action Planner: Custom milestone pathways.

What Good Products Do

  • Explain why recommendations are shown using dataset logic.
  • Offer actionable pathways instead of overwhelming skill sets.
Creative Track

Open Track

Unleash your creativity. Build anything useful and playful, or "boliao but harmless". Life is beautiful in its diversity and inclusion — and so is Python.

Build Ideas

  • Playful Utilities: Interactive apps, custom games, or funny Python mods.
  • Community Projects: Visualizations, helper tools, or automation hacks.

Who is welcome

  • Students, retirees, career builders, and developers of all skill levels.
  • The focus is on practical learning, positive community outcomes, and sharing Python.
